Detailed Description

This class represents a passing car. This class is derived from

See also
Entity. A passing car has:
  • A shared pointer of the player.
  • A shared pointer to the world.
  • A boolean to determine whether te car can be destroyed or not.

Determine whether the PassingCar can be destroyed or not. A PassingCar can be destroyed if it's Y position is lower than 2 times the minimum Y or if it's Y position is greater than 2 times the maximum Y.
